# DTI Remix — Changelog

## June 30, 2026

### Fixes
- **Closet:** Your unfiled items now show under "Not in a list," and you can drag them straight into any list to sort them.
- **Everywhere:** The Log out button works.
- **Customize:** Sorting search results by Oldest or Newest first works.
- **Customize:** Search finds items even if you type the words out of order or skip punctuation like apostrophes and colons.
- **Customize:** The Fitting Room list stays neatly inside its card instead of spilling over the edge.
- **Closet:** The selection toolbar no longer covers the clipboard bar.

### Enhancements
- **Pet Style picker:** Double-click the bar at the top of the picker to pop it out into a floating window. Drag that bar to move it around, drag the bottom-right corner to resize it, and double-click the bar again (or click the pin) to dock it back. Now you can browse styles while watching your pet.
- **Pet Style picker:** The "+ Add to Wants" button now sits in the top-left corner of each style — click it to add that style to your Wants. (It used to be easy to hit by accident while scrolling.)
- **Customize:** Pose thumbnails zoom in on your pet's face, so each expression is easy to tell apart.
- **Customize:** The Mood selector is simpler now — it just shows the expression (Happy, Sad, or Sick).
- **Closet:** If you don't have any lists yet, a hint now shows you where to make your first one.
